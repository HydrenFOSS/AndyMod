# AndyMod

![AndyMod Banner](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/HydrenFOSS/AndyMod/refs/heads/main/banner.png)

Andy is a person who just wants to play with you. He seems like a perfectly fine helper at first, but as the days progress, he starts to get corrupted...

## Spawning Andy

To bring Andy into your world, you must build a specific ritual structure:

1. Lay down a **3x3 platform of Cobblestone**.
2. Place **Torches** on the outer edges around the center block.
3. Light a **Fire** directly in the dead center.

> ⚠️ **Important:** Make sure to light the fire at *last* to complete the summon.

---

## Configuration & API Setup

AndyMod requires an API key from an AI provider to function. Supported providers include **OpenAI**, **Gemini**, and **Groq** (Groq is highly recommended for the best experience).

### Client/Server Side Setup

If you are playing on the client side, you must add your API key to your local configuration file.

1. Navigate to your Minecraft/Server directory.
2. Open `config/andymod-common.toml` in a text editor.
3. Insert your key and provider details

--- 

### Following Providers are supported:

1. groq (not grok)
2. openai
3. gemini
4. local 

* To use the local AI, please refer to the [AndyMod-LocalAI Repository](https://github.com/HydrenFOSS/AndyMod-LocalAI.git).

## Features

* **An Evolving Companion:** Andy starts as a helpful partner to accompany you on your adventures.
* **The Corruption:** Watch the environment and Andy's behavior twist as time goes on.
* **Dynamic AI Interactions:** Powered by LLMs to make Andy feel uniquely alive.
